About Us



Founded in 2018, Chapel Hill, our mission at SpeakEasy is to help connect job seekers with budding industry professionals while providing a safe space for our members to get an unfiltered perspective on what matters to them most.

At the heart of our success and future is transparency: We are changing the way people connect and think about work, and want to give you access to all of the details that are often lurking in the shadows.
